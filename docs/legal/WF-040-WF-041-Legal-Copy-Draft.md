## Privacy Policy Draft

### Privacy Policy

Effective date: [TBD]

waliFit helps users plan training, log workouts and runs, track simple nutrition habits, grow a Vitality Tree, and use Wali AI coaching features. This Privacy Policy explains what information waliFit collects, how it is used, and the choices users have.

This policy applies to the waliFit mobile app, website, backend services, and related support channels.

### Information We Collect

We collect information users provide directly, information generated while using the app, and limited information from device permissions users choose to enable.

Account information:
- Email address, authentication identifiers, display name, username, profile photo, account status, legal acceptance timestamps, and app preferences.
- Sign-in information from supported providers such as Apple, Google, or email authentication.

Profile and training setup:
- Goals, training experience, preferred units, training days, available equipment, injuries or limitations users choose to enter, body weight, daily protein target, daily hydration target, and rest timer defaults.

Workout, run, and nutrition information:
- Planned workouts, completed workouts, sets, reps, weights, RPE, workout notes, exercise history, personal records, calendar entries, protein and hydration logs, and related progress data.
- WaliRun data, including active-run location data used to calculate route, distance, pace, duration, splits, and run personal records.
- Step count read from Apple Health, Google Fit, or Health Connect when the user grants permission. waliFit uses steps for the Vitality Tree activity score. Steps are not manually entered.

AI and coaching information:
- Messages sent to Wali AI, generated coaching responses, plan-generation prompts, training context used to personalize responses, and safety-related metadata needed to operate AI features.
- Nutrition parsing inputs and photos if the user chooses to use photo-based or screenshot-based import features.

Social and Arena information:
- Squad membership, leaderboard activity, PR feed activity, run club participation, friends, messages, and display information needed for social features.

Device, diagnostic, and usage information:
- Device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, error logs, performance diagnostics, and basic app events needed to operate, secure, debug, and improve waliFit.

Support and communications:
- Messages users send to support, feedback, deletion requests, export requests, and other communications with waliFit.

### Device Permissions

waliFit only uses device permissions for specific app features:

- Health and fitness data: to read step count for the Vitality Tree activity pillar.
- Location while using the app: to track active WaliRun sessions. waliFit does not track location in the background for V1.
- Camera or photo library: to upload a profile photo, import a workout screenshot, or use planned photo-based nutrition features when enabled.
- Notifications: to send workout reminders, coaching messages, streak reminders, and account or safety notices when the user allows notifications.

Users can change device permissions in their operating system settings. Some app features may not work without the related permission.

### How We Use Information

waliFit uses information to:

- Create and manage user accounts.
- Provide workout logging, run tracking, simple nutrition logging, calendar, Vitality Tree, streak, rest timer, and Arena features.
- Generate AI coaching responses, training plans, workout adjustments, and context-aware recommendations.
- Sync data across devices and restore user progress.
- Provide data export and account deletion.
- Personalize the app experience, including units, targets, training context, and notifications.
- Detect abuse, protect accounts, enforce Terms, debug issues, and maintain service reliability.
- Comply with legal, safety, App Store, and platform requirements.

waliFit does not sell personal information. waliFit does not use personal information for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

### AI Providers and Data Processing

Some Wali AI features send user inputs and relevant training context to third-party AI providers so they can generate a response.

Planned V1 provider routing:

- Anthropic: Wali AI coaching messages, complex training questions, program-generation prompts, and relevant training context.
- Google: nutrition parsing, photo or screenshot parsing when enabled, simple questions, onboarding import, and other high-volume parsing tasks.
- waliFit servers: account data, logs, runs, nutrition entries, squad activity, messages, AI routing metadata, and sync records.
- On device: rest timer, exercise library access, offline log queue, local cache, active run capture before sync, and local Vitality Tree display state.

waliFit aims to send only the information reasonably needed for the requested AI feature. AI providers may process data under their own terms and privacy documentation. Users should not enter information into Wali AI that they do not want processed by waliFit or its AI providers.

### AI Training Opt-Out

V1 includes an AI training opt-out setting. When enabled, waliFit will not use that user's personal content to improve waliFit-owned models, prompts, evaluations, or product training datasets, except as needed for security, safety, abuse prevention, legal compliance, and service operation.

The opt-out does not prevent data from being processed to provide the AI feature the user requests. It also does not control whether third-party AI providers retain or process data under their own service terms. waliFit should verify provider retention and training settings before launch.

### Data Sharing

waliFit may share information with:

- Service providers that host, store, process, secure, analyze, or support the app.
- AI providers, including Anthropic and Google, when users use AI-powered features.
- Authentication providers, such as Apple, Google, or email authentication services.
- App platforms, such as Apple App Store and Google Play, as needed for distribution, safety, compliance, and platform reporting.
- Legal, safety, or compliance recipients when required by law or necessary to protect users, waliFit, or others.
- Other users only when the user uses social features, such as squads, leaderboards, PR feed posts, run clubs, friends, or messages.

waliFit does not sell user data to advertisers.

### Data Retention

waliFit keeps information while an account is active and as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and enforce Terms.

When a user deletes an account, waliFit immediately deactivates the account and schedules hard deletion of user data after 30 days, unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance reasons.

### Data Export and Account Deletion

Users can request a JSON export of their waliFit data from Settings. The planned export includes account settings, profile data, workout logs, run logs, nutrition logs, Vitality Tree state, calendar records, AI-related records, and social data associated with the account.

Users can delete their account in Settings. Account deletion is intended to deactivate the account immediately and permanently delete eligible data after 30 days.

### User Choices

Users can:

- Update profile and preference information in the app.
- Enable or disable notification permissions.
- Grant or revoke device permissions in operating system settings.
- Export their data as JSON.
- Delete their account.
- Opt out of waliFit using personal content to improve waliFit AI training or evaluation datasets.
- Stop using AI features if they do not want their messages or context processed by AI providers.

### Children

waliFit is not intended for children under 13. Users under the age required by their country or region must have permission from a parent or legal guardian. waliFit should confirm minimum-age and consent requirements before launch.

### Security

waliFit uses reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect user information. No system is perfectly secure, and users should protect their account credentials and avoid sending sensitive medical, financial, or highly confidential information through Wali AI.

### International Processing

waliFit and its providers may process information in the United States and other countries where service providers operate. Privacy laws may differ from the laws in the user's location.

### Changes to This Policy

waliFit may update this Privacy Policy as features, providers, or legal requirements change. When changes are material, waliFit will provide notice through the app or another appropriate method.

### Contact

Questions, privacy requests, data export requests, or deletion issues can be sent to:

[privacy@walifit.app]

## Terms of Service Draft

### Terms of Service

Effective date: [TBD]

These Terms of Service govern use of the waliFit mobile app, website, backend services, Wali AI features, and related services. By creating an account or using waliFit, the user agrees to these Terms.

This is product/legal draft copy and must be reviewed before publication.

### Eligibility

Users must be at least 13 years old or the minimum age required in their country or region. If a user is under the age of majority, they may use waliFit only with permission from a parent or legal guardian.

### waliFit Is Not Medical Advice

waliFit is a fitness, training, habit, and coaching-support app. waliFit, Wali AI, training plans, workout adjustments, nutrition guidance, run metrics, Vitality Tree feedback, and social features do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, physical therapy, clinical nutrition advice, or emergency services.

Users are responsible for deciding whether training, running, diet, hydration, supplements, or lifestyle changes are appropriate for them. Users should consult a qualified medical, fitness, or nutrition professional before starting or changing a fitness program, especially if they have a medical condition, injury, pain, pregnancy, medication use, eating disorder history, or other health concern.

### Assumption of Risk

Exercise, running, strength training, conditioning, diet changes, hydration changes, and using fitness advice involve risks, including injury, illness, overtraining, dehydration, or other harm. By using waliFit, users understand and accept these risks.

Users should stop exercising and seek professional help if they experience chest pain, fainting, severe shortness of breath, sudden weakness, severe pain, symptoms of heat illness, signs of injury, or any other concerning symptoms.

### Emergency and Safety Boundaries

waliFit is not an emergency service. Users should call local emergency services immediately for urgent or life-threatening situations. Wali AI should not be used for crisis support, medical emergencies, urgent injury assessment, medication decisions, diagnosis, or treatment.

### User Accounts

Users are responsible for maintaining accurate account information, safeguarding login credentials, and all activity under their account. Users must notify waliFit if they believe their account has been compromised.

### Acceptable Use

Users may not:

- Use waliFit for unlawful, harmful, abusive, deceptive, or unsafe purposes.
- Attempt to reverse engineer, scrape, overload, attack, or interfere with waliFit systems.
- Upload malware or content that violates another person's rights.
- Misrepresent identity, impersonate others, or abuse social features.
- Use Wali AI to seek instructions for self-harm, harming others, evading law enforcement, dangerous substance use, illegal conduct, or other unsafe behavior.
- Rely on waliFit as a substitute for professional medical, mental health, legal, financial, or nutrition advice.

### User Content

Users may enter workout notes, messages, profile content, photos, training goals, health-related context, and other content into waliFit. Users retain ownership of their content, but grant waliFit permission to host, process, display, transmit, analyze, and use it as needed to provide, secure, improve, and support the service, subject to privacy settings and the Privacy Policy.

Users are responsible for the content they submit and must have rights to submit it.

### AI Features

Wali AI responses may be incomplete, inaccurate, outdated, or inappropriate for a user's circumstances. AI output should be treated as informational training support, not professional advice. Users should independently evaluate AI suggestions and consult qualified professionals for health decisions.

waliFit may route AI requests to third-party providers, including Anthropic and Google. AI features may use profile, workout, nutrition, run, calendar, Vitality Tree, and social context to personalize responses. Users can opt out of waliFit using personal content for waliFit AI training or evaluation datasets, but AI processing may still occur to provide requested AI features.

### Social Features

Arena, squads, leaderboards, PR feeds, run clubs, friends, and messages may share user activity with other users. Users should avoid posting sensitive information and should use respectful conduct. waliFit may remove content, restrict accounts, or disable social features to protect users and the service.

### Data Export and Account Deletion

Users may request a JSON export of their data and may delete their account through Settings. Account deletion is intended to immediately deactivate the account and permanently delete eligible data after 30 days, subject to legal, safety, security, fraud-prevention, and compliance retention needs.

### Subscriptions and Payments

V1 is planned to be free. If waliFit adds paid features, subscriptions, or purchases later, additional payment terms will apply and will be presented before purchase.

### Changes to the Service

waliFit may modify, suspend, or discontinue features, providers, integrations, or availability. waliFit may update these Terms as features or legal requirements change. Continued use after updated Terms become effective means the user accepts the updated Terms.

### Termination

waliFit may suspend or terminate access if a user violates these Terms, creates risk for other users, abuses the service, or uses waliFit unlawfully. Users may stop using waliFit at any time and may delete their account in Settings.

### Disclaimers

waliFit is provided "as is" and "as available." To the maximum extent allowed by law, waliFit disclaims warranties of merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose, non-infringement, accuracy, availability, and uninterrupted operation.

### Limitation of Liability

## AI Disclosure Screen Copy Draft

### Screen Title

AI & Data Disclosure

### Short Intro

waliFit uses a mix of on-device features, waliFit servers, and third-party AI providers. This screen explains what stays on your device, what is sent to AI providers, and what is stored by waliFit.

### Persistent Safety Banner

Wali AI is a training assistant, not a medical professional. It does not diagnose, treat injuries, prescribe medication, or provide emergency help. For health decisions, pain, injury, medical conditions, or nutrition concerns, consult a qualified professional. For emergencies, call local emergency services.

### On Device

These features are designed to run on your phone before sync or without sending the raw activity to an AI provider:

- Active GPS capture during WaliRun before the run is saved.
- Rest timer.
- Vitality Tree display state, streak display, and local cache.
- Exercise library browsing.
- Offline workout and nutrition logging queue.
- Basic app preferences and cached screens.

Some on-device data may sync to waliFit servers when you save, reconnect, or use account-based features.

### Sent to Anthropic

Used for complex Wali AI coaching and training-plan tasks:

- Wali AI chat messages.
- Program Architect prompts.
- Recent workout history, run history, training goals, schedule, equipment, limitations, and nutrition context needed to answer your request.
- Safety-relevant context, such as pain or injury words you choose to send in a message.

Anthropic responses may be wrong or incomplete. Do not use Wali AI for diagnosis, treatment, medication decisions, injury clearance, or emergency guidance.

### Sent to Google

Used for parsing and lower-complexity AI tasks:

- Nutrition parsing.
- Food photos when photo-based nutrition features are enabled.
- Workout or fitness-app screenshots when you choose import features.
- Simple Wali AI questions that do not require complex coaching.
- Onboarding import data when you choose to upload screenshots.

Google AI responses or parsed results may be wrong. Review imported or parsed data before relying on it.

### Sent to waliFit Servers

Stored and processed by waliFit to operate your account and sync features:

- Account profile, settings, legal acceptance timestamps, and AI training opt-out preference.
- Workout logs, run logs, nutrition logs, rest timer settings, calendar entries, and Vitality Tree data.
- Wali AI messages, AI routing metadata, and generated coaching records needed to show your history and improve safety.
- Squad activity, leaderboards, PR feed activity, run club activity, friends, and messages.
- Data export, account deletion, support, security, diagnostic, and crash-related records.

### AI Training Opt-Out Copy

Label: Opt out of AI training

Description: When this is on, waliFit will not use your personal content to improve waliFit AI training, prompt evaluation, or product datasets. Your data may still be processed to provide the AI feature you request, keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and meet legal requirements.

Inline note: This setting does not stop Wali AI from processing your message when you use AI features. It also does not replace third-party AI provider privacy controls.

### Limits of Wali AI

Wali AI can help with training ideas, workout structure, general fitness education, habit reflection, and plan adjustments. It cannot:

- Diagnose injuries, illnesses, or medical conditions.
- Tell you whether pain is safe to train through.
- Prescribe, stop, or adjust medications.
- Replace a doctor, physical therapist, registered dietitian, coach, or emergency service.
- Guarantee results, personal records, weight loss, muscle gain, or injury prevention.
- Reliably detect every unsafe request or incorrect answer.

### Emergency and Injury Boundary

If you have chest pain, fainting, severe shortness of breath, sudden weakness, severe pain, signs of heat illness, a possible fracture, head injury, or any urgent concern, stop using the app and contact emergency services or a qualified professional.
